# Product Roadmap (High-Level)

## Roadmap Purpose

This document outlines the high-level product development plan from initial setup through MVP delivery and subsequent versions. The roadmap is focused on product iterations rather than low-level tasks.

---

## Iteration 0 — Foundation & Setup

**Goal:** Establish a stable foundation for further development.

- Repository initialization.
- Next.js (App Router) + TypeScript setup.
- Linting, formatting, and CI configuration.
- Definition of base architecture.
- Storyblok space and content model setup.

---

## Iteration 1 — Content Delivery MVP

**Goal:** Deliver reference content from the CMS.

- Storyblok integration.
- Core reference content pages.
- Static page generation.
- Minimal UI implementation.

---

## Iteration 2 — Navigation & Search

**Goal:** Enable fast and intuitive access to content.

- Primary navigation.
- Breadcrumbs.
- Full-text search.

---

## Iteration 3 — i18n & Performance

**Goal:** Support multilingual usage and stable performance.

- RU/EN localization.
- Language-aware routing.
- Caching and load optimization.

---

## Iteration 4 — Quality & Public Readiness

**Goal:** Prepare the MVP for public exposure.

- Testing of critical flows.
- Documentation (README, docs).
- Final UI polish.
- Public deployment.

---

## Post-MVP (V1 / V2)

- Accounts and personalization.
- Notes and bookmarks.
- GM Board.
- Advanced search and filtering.
